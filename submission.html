Submission and Reviews

The review process of the workshop is double blind at the level of Program Committee members (PC) and the authors. Our submission requirements are followed by the paper submission requirements of ACM Multimedia 2021.

The review process does not involve authors feedback and rebuttals. Upon acceptance, the program chairs/committees will select several papers as oral presentations in the workshop, as well as the best paper award among all the submissions.

Scope

This workshop targets researchers and practitioners from both industry and academia to provide a forum in which to publish recent state-of-the-art achievements in the Multimedia Understanding with Less Labeling area. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Few/Zero-shot learning in Multimedia
  • Self-supervised learning in Multimedia
  • Semi-supervised learning in Multimedia
  • Long-tailed distribution learning in Multimedia
  • Weakly-supervised learning in Multimedia
  • Active learning in Multimedia
  • Incremental/Continual learning in Multimedia
  • Multimodal learning with small/ Incomplete data
